Frequently Asked Questions
How is Ecficio pronounced?
“Ek-fi-cho”
Is Ecficio part of Convergence?
No, they are two completely separate fests. Ecficio is an Entrepreneurship Fest while Convergence is a Technical Fest. !!!
What do you mean by Ecficio is Hybrid?
A hybrid fest is held both offline - on the VNRVJIET Campus - as well as online
What are the prerequisites?
For the events, there are no prerequisites at all. You can just come, enjoy and learn. For the competitions, you may need to form your teams beforehand. Check the description of each competition for more details.
How can I register for the fest?
Approach us at our registration desks (infront of B-block and Hungry Jacks) to proceed with the payment.
Do we have to register for each event and competition?
No, all events and competitions are available to anyone who registered for the Fest as a whole.
What is the Entry Fee?
The entry fee is Rs 300 if you register alone . There is a discount of Rs 50 if you register as a group ( So the ticket costs Rs 250 ).
